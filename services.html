Post-TAVR Cardiac Monitoring with Live-Streaming ECG

Post-TAVR Monitoring, Built for Continuity

Patients recovering from TAVR remain at elevated risk for delayed conduction abnormalities and arrhythmias, making reliable post-procedure cardiac monitoring essential. Specialized Medical's MCT program pairs the S-Patch ECG monitor with a resilient, multi-path cellular transmission platform designed for continuous, live-streaming ECG data — even in rural and lower-coverage environments. This supports consistent ECG capture and faster physician awareness of actionable rhythm changes throughout the post-TAVR monitoring period.
